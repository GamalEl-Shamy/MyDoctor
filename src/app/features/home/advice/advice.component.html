Tips for Staying Healthy

Eat a Balanced Diet

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your daily meals.

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 7–8 glasses of water every day to keep your body active and refreshed.

Exercise Regularly

Aim for at least 30 minutes of physical activity, 4–5 times a week.

Get Enough Sleep

Try to sleep 7–8 hours every night to recharge your body and mind.

Avoid Smoking
